### **1. Fundamental Economic Concepts**

- **Scarcity and Opportunity Cost** – The foundation of economic decision-making
- **Supply and Demand** – Market equilibrium, price elasticity
- **Marginal Analysis** – Diminishing marginal returns, decision-making at the margin
- **Incentives and Rational Choice Theory** – How people respond to costs and benefits

---

### **2. Microeconomics (Individual and Firm-Level Analysis)**

- **Consumer Behavior** – Utility theory, indifference curves
- **Production and Costs** – Short-run vs. long-run costs, economies of scale
- **Market Structures** – Perfect competition, monopoly, oligopoly, monopolistic competition
- **Game Theory** – Nash equilibrium, strategic interactions
- **Factor Markets** – Labor, capital, land markets, and wage determination

---

### **3. Macroeconomics (Economy-Wide Analysis)**

- **Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and Economic Growth** – Measurement, growth models
- **Inflation and Deflation** – Causes, measurement, and effects
- **Unemployment** – Types (frictional, structural, cyclical), natural rate of unemployment
- **Business Cycles** – Expansion, recession, recovery phases
- **Monetary Policy** – Central banks, money supply, interest rates
- **Fiscal Policy** – Government spending, taxation, budget deficits

---

### **4. International Economics**

- **Trade Theories** – Comparative advantage, absolute advantage
- **Exchange Rates and Currency Markets** – Fixed vs. floating exchange rates
- **Balance of Payments** – Current account, capital account
- **Globalization and Trade Agreements** – WTO, NAFTA, EU, tariffs, and quotas

---

### **5. Development and Political Economy**

- **Economic Development** – Growth vs. development, HDI, poverty reduction
- **Institutions and Governance** – Role of property rights, corruption, economic freedom
- **Inequality and Redistribution** – Gini coefficient, progressive taxation
- **Public Goods and Market Failures** – Externalities, public choice theory

---

### **6. Financial Economics**

- **Stock Markets and Investment** – Efficient markets hypothesis, behavioral finance
- **Banking and Financial Institutions** – Role of banks, central banking, shadow banking
- **Risk and Uncertainty** – Portfolio theory, derivatives, insurance markets
- **Cryptocurrencies and Digital Finance** – Bitcoin, DeFi, fintech

---

### **7. Behavioral and Experimental Economics**

- **Cognitive Biases in Decision-Making** – Prospect theory, loss aversion
- **Nudging and Policy Design** – Applications in public policy and regulation
- **Game Theory in Practice** – Auctions, voting behavior

---

### **8. Environmental and Resource Economics**

- **Sustainable Growth** – Green GDP, circular economy
- **Energy Economics** – Pricing, subsidies, renewable transitions
- **Climate Change Economics** – Carbon pricing, cap-and-trade systems
